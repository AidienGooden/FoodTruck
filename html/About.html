About Us

At Sweet Serenade, we believe that ice cream is more than just a dessert; That's why we've made it our mission to serve up the most delicious, handcrafted ice cream treats on the streets of our city. Sweet Serenade is more than just an ice cream truck; it's a mobile oasis of fun and joy. With our vibrant, eye-catching truck and friendly staff, Whether you're a kid at heart or just looking to beat the heat, you'll find a warm welcome and a cold treat at Sweet Serenade. So join us on our sweet adventure! Every customer is part of our family.
